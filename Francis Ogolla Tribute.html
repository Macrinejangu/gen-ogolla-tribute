Francis Ogolla Tribute to General Francis Ogolla

General Francis Omondi Ogolla (1960-2024)

Chief of defence forces, Kenya

Introduction

General Francis Ogolla was a highly respected Kenyan military officer who served as the Chief of Defence Forces from April 2023 until his passing in 2024. He was known for his dedication to duty, integrity, and patriotism.

Key Accomplishments

Here are some of his most notable achievements:

  • Joined the Kenya Defence Forces in 1984 and was commissioned as a 2nd Lieutenant in 1985.
  • Trained as a fighter pilot in the U.S. and became an instructor pilot at the Kenya Air Force.
  • Served in the former Yugoslavia as an Observer and Military Information Officer (1992–1993).
  • Held leadership roles including Commander of the Kenya Air Force and Vice Chief of Defence Forces.
  • Appointed Chief of Defence Forces in April 2023 by President William Ruto.
  • Awarded honors such as Elder of the Order of the Golden Heart (EGH) and Chief of the Order of the Burning Spear (CBS).

Notable Quotes

"Our motherland has lost one of her most valiant generals." – President William Ruto

"A distinguished four-star general has fallen in the course of duty, and service to country." – President William Ruto

Legacy

General Ogolla will be remembered for his professionalism, humility, and service to the Republic of Kenya. His legacy lives on in the officers he mentored and the strength he brought to the defence forces.
